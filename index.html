About ASC Dupe Checker

Our story and mission

ASC Dupe Checker was developed to address duplicate payments in ASC payment files. By identifying duplicate payments before they're processed, this tool helps organizations prevent duplicate payment records.

The application is optimized for the Reconciliation department handling ASC payment files and provides a robust solution for ensuring payment integrity.
